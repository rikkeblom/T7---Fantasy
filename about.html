Welcome to our Character Generator

If you are seeking fantastical inspiration, our generator will come with great haste to your aide.

What can character-creation be used for?

The characters can be used in fantasy role-play gaming (such as Dungeons and Dragons) or to use in writing or visual art.

What is the ethos behind the site?

This site is a free resource for all who want to escape into other realms. We are very much copy-left and our fantasy-genre-related advertising is used sparingly.

Who are the makers of this site?

We are four students in Multimedia Design and this is a project that was set on the topic of shared interest. We chose to make a site based on the fantasy genre - because each of us either role-plays, reads, writes or creates art based on fantasy-concepts and world-building.
